## What is This?

This is a browser-based arcade game where you control a shark with an unusual craving for tropical fruit. Navigate the deep blue sea, catch falling pineapples, dodge explosive bombs, and see how high you can score before things get... explosive.

## How to Play

1. Open [shark-game.html](shark-game.html) in your web browser
2. Use the **‚Üê ‚Üí** arrow keys to move your shark left and right
3. Catch the falling pineapples to increase your score
4. **AVOID THE BOMBS** or face a dramatic underwater explosion
5. Survive as long as you can and rack up those points!

## Features

- **Beautiful Ocean Graphics**: Smooth gradient backgrounds with rising bubbles for that authentic underwater feel
- **Detailed Shark Animation**: A shark with proper teeth, fins, and even direction-aware sprites
- **Power-ups**: Eat 10 pineapples to earn a protective shield that blocks one bomb
- **Death Animation**: When things go wrong, they go BOOM with particle effects
- **Score Tracking**: Keep track of your pineapple-eating prowess
- **Instant Restart**: Try again immediately with the click of a button

## Game Mechanics

- **Pineapples** (+10 points): Your main objective. Catch as many as you can!
- **Bombs** (instant death): One hit and it's game over... unless you have a shield
- **Shield**: Automatically awarded every 10 pineapples eaten. Saves you from one bomb hit
- **Speed**: Both pineapples and bombs fall at varying speeds to keep you on your fins
